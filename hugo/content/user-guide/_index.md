## Supported Document Formats

Cheetah supports the following document formats:

- **Plain Text** (.txt)
- **Markdown** (.md, .markdown)
- **PDF** (.pdf)
- **Microsoft Word** (.docx)
- **EPUB** (.epub)
- **OpenDocument Text** (.odt)

## Speed Settings

Reading speed is measured in Words Per Minute (WPM):

| Speed | WPM | Best For |
|-------|-----|----------|
| Relaxed | 100-200 | Learning RSVP, difficult texts |
| Normal | 200-350 | Regular reading |
| Fast | 350-500 | Familiar topics |
| Very Fast | 500-700 | Skimming, easy content |
| Speed Demon | 700-1000+ | Maximum speed |

## Tips for Speed Reading

1. **Start slow**: Begin at 200-300 WPM and gradually increase
2. **Stay focused**: Minimize distractions
3. **Don't subvocalize**: Train yourself not to "speak" words internally
4. **Take breaks**: Reading at high speeds is mentally intensive
5. **Practice regularly**: Your speed will improve over time

## Session Persistence

Cheetah automatically saves your reading position for each document. When you return to a document, you'll be offered the option to resume from where you left off.

Settings are stored in:
- **TUI**: `~/.config/cheetah/`
- **Web**: Browser localStorage
